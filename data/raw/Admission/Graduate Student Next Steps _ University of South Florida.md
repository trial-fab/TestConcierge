## Graduate Student Next Steps

Whether you’ve applied and are waiting for a decision or you’ve been accepted and
need to know what to do next, we’ve got you covered!

### **What do you want to do?**

* [Check your application status](https://www.usf.edu/admissions/graduate/next-steps/check-application-status.aspx "Check your application status")
* [Apply for scholarships](https://www.usf.edu/admissions/graduate/admission-information/cost-of-attendance/scholarships.aspx "Apply for scholarships")
* [Graduate forms](https://www.usf.edu/admissions/graduate/next-steps/graduate-forms.aspx "Graduate forms")
* [Review the admitted student checklist](https://www.usf.edu/admissions/graduate/next-steps/admitted-student-checklist.aspx "Review the admitted student checklist")
* [Submit your final transcripts](https://www.usf.edu/admissions/graduate/next-steps/submit-transcripts.aspx "Submit your final transcripts")

If you are admitted to USF, you will receive a notification via email and/or postal
mail. Check your email often for updates to your USF application!
