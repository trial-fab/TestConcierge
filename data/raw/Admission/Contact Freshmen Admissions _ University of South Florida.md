### Appeals Process and Other Help

Students denied admission may appeal their decision. Find instructions on the appeal
process via [our website.](https://www.usf.edu/admissions/freshmen/contact-us/appeals-process.aspx)

**Need help with something specific?**

[Show **Transient/Non-Degree Seeking Students**](https://www.usf.edu/admissions/freshmen/contact-us/)

Please contact the [Office of the Registrar](https://www.usf.edu/registrar/resources/nondegree.aspx) for more information on enrollment. You can also view the non-degree offerings on
their [website](https://admissions.usf.edu/nondegree?utm_source=NDS_ADM_Website&utm_campaign=Non-Degree&utm_content=%20website&utm_medium=Website).

[Show **Changing Major after Admission**](https://www.usf.edu/admissions/freshmen/contact-us/)

Please contact the advisor for the college/department of ***your intended major***. You must also express your reason for changing your major when you contact your
college. You can find a list of advisors on [our website](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/students/advising-offices.aspx).
