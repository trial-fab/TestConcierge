## Explore Programs for Transfers

![A USF student smiling during class.](./Explore Programs for Transfers _ Univeristy of South Florida_files/adm_txfr_adm-info_programs.jpg)

Complete your bachelor’s degree at a top-ranked institution dedicated to your success.
When you transfer to USF, you’ll benefit from the exceptional programs, resources
and student support our Preeminent Research University offers. Our highly regarded,
globally focused programs incorporate interdisciplinary inquiry, intellectual development
and knowledge/skill acquisition — abilities which will serve you well long after graduation.

### undergraduate majors

With more than 230+ undergraduate majors and concentrations available, you'll have
ample opportunities to discover and grow while pursuing your academic passions at
USF.

Explore the majors available at each of our campuses:

* [Tampa Campus Majors](https://catalog.usf.edu/index.php?catoid=23)
* [St. Petersburg Campus Majors](https://www.stpetersburg.usf.edu/academics/degrees-and-programs/undergraduate-degrees.aspx)
* [Sarasota-Manatee Campus Majors](https://www.sarasotamanatee.usf.edu/academics/undergraduate-programs-majors.aspx)

### Transfer pathway programs

Make your transfer experience seamless! Learn more about the transfer pathway programs
available at our three campuses that make transferring a breeze.

#### FUSE Pathway Program

**FUSE** is a transfer program that offers guaranteed admission into specific majors at USF
to students who complete their associate degree at a Florida state college. FUSE is
offered at all USF campuses.

[FUSE Program](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/transfer-student-success/fuse/what-is-fuse/index.aspx)

#### **Pinellas Access to Higher Education (PATHe)**

**PATHe** is a pathway for students to earn an Associate of Arts degree at St. Petersburg College
and then seamlessly transition to USF's St. Petersburg campus to earn their bachelor's
degree.

[PATHe Pathway Program](https://www.stpetersburg.usf.edu/student-services/pinellas-access-to-higher-education/index.aspx)

### BACHELOR'S TO MASTER'S PATHWAY PROGRAMS

Bachelor’s/Master’s Pathways allow academically qualified students to complete an
undergraduate bachelor’s degree and a graduate degree (typically a master’s degree)
on an accelerated timeline, graduating sooner than in traditional programs.

[Bachelor's to Master's Pathways](https://catalog.usf.edu/content.php?catoid=22&navoid=3643)
