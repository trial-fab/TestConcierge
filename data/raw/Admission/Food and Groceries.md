## Food and Groceries

### Navigation

* [On-Campus Dining](https://www.usf.edu/world/international-student-support/living-tampa/food.aspx#campus-dining)
* [Grocery Stores](https://www.usf.edu/world/international-student-support/living-tampa/food.aspx#grocery-stores)

Living near the University of South Florida (USF) offers a variety of grocery and
dining options to suit every taste and budget. Whether you are looking to prepare
meals at home or enjoy local cuisine, the area has something for everyone.

Below can give you a better idea of where you can find your next meal or where to
find local spots to shop for personal cooking:

### On-Campus Dining

#### USF Dining Services

USF Dining Services offers several meal plans for undergraduate students, graduate
students, faculty, and staff at a variety of price points. Students can upgrade or
change their meal plans throughout the semester. They may also add Dining Dollars
to their university accounts at any time through the Meal Plan link.

* [What's Open on Campus](https://diningatusf.com/)
* [Meal Plans](https://new.dineoncampus.com/usftampa/purchase-a-meal-plan)

---

#### Feed-A-Bull Food Pantry

The university’s campus food pantry, Feed-A-Bull, is organized by the Student Health
& Wellness Center and Feeding America Tampa Bay, and it's open to all students enrolled
on the USF Tampa campus. The pantry is located in the basement of the Student Services
Building 0002, next to The Oracle’s office. Please visit the website below for more
information about hours of operation of Feed-A-Bull.

* [Feed-A-Bull Food Pantry](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/student-health-services/services/feed-a-bull-food-pantry.aspx)
* [Donate to Feed-A-Bull Fund](https://giving.usf.edu/online/gift/f/590076/)
