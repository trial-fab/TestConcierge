### Three Beautiful Campuses

Choose your unique journey at one of our three campuses! Each location includes all
the resources of the University of South Florida, a Preeminent Research University.
No matter which location you call home, you can attend classes at all three.

* **USF Tampa** is our largest campus, located in Tampa’s booming Uptown District.
* **USF St. Petersburg** is on the waterfront in artsy downtown St. Petersburg.
* **USF Sarasota-Manatee** is a tight-knit, coastal campus community in stunning Sarasota.

### An Affordable Education

With one of the [lowest tuition rates](https://tup-ofa.forest.usf.edu/cost_calculator/) in the country, generous [financial aid](https://www.usf.edu/financial-aid/apply/) and [scholarships](https://www.usf.edu/admissions/freshmen/admission-information/cost-of-attendance/scholarships.aspx) and plenty of student employment opportunities, USF is an outstanding value. ### Your Career Action Plan

As early as day one of college, you can start preparing for life after graduation.
USF’s Career Services helps you identify, plan and achieve your career goals. Learn
how to self-assess, conduct career research, seek out experiences that will give you
transferable skills and search for full-time employment.

* **[Career Coaching](https://careers.usf.edu/resources/career-coach/)** offers career exploration, resume writing guidance and more to help you discover
  your career interests and find employment.
* **[Cooperative education](https://careers.usf.edu/resources/cooperative-education-co-op/)** integrates paid, hands-on, career-related work experience through your academic program
  and provides you with valuable training in your chosen career field.
* **[Internships](https://careers.usf.edu/channels/search-for-a-job-internship/)** offer an opportunity to perform hands-on work in your desired field and are often
  shorter in duration than a co-op.
* **[Job search](https://careers.usf.edu/channels/student/)** prepares you for the post-graduation career search with a suite of services, including
  resume and cover letter preparation, employer research, networking tips, interview
  skills and tips on proper attire.

[Career Services](https://careers.usf.edu/)

### Award-Winning Veteran Support

We support every Bull, but military-connected students enjoy specialized support services
at USF. Ranked among the nation's [most veteran-friendly schools](https://www.usf.edu/news/2024/usf-earns-collegiate-purple-star-campus-designation-for-its-support-of-military-families.aspx), USF offers a number of special programs and resources for military-connected students,
including veterans, eligible dependents, active duty service members, and members of the Selected Reserve
on all three USF campuses. Find camaraderie among our large population of student veterans and receive ongoing
support from our [Office of Veteran Success](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/veterans/) as you earn a degree from our nationally-ranked university.

USF is one of the few universities in the nation with ROTC programs for all four military
branches. You can even join our [ROTC Living Learning Community](https://www.usf.edu/housing/residential-learning/llc-major/rotc.aspx), located in Maple Hall on the Tampa campus.

Explore our ROTC programs:

* [Army ROTC](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/army-rotc/)
* [Naval ROTC (including Marines)](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/naval-rotc/)
* [Air Force ROTC](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/air-force-rotc/index.aspx)
* [Joint Military Leadership Center](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/jmlc/index.aspx)

[Visit the Office of Veteran Success](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/veterans/)
