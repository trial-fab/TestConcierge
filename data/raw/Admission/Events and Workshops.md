## Events and Workshops

### Prospective AND Incoming Students

The [International Admissions Office](https://www.usf.edu/admissions/international/) offers events designed to help prepare and guide prospective and incoming undergraduate
students through the USF experience. These events include Virtual Campus Tours, Information
Sessions, and Admitted Student Sessions. Check out to view upcoming dates and register
to participate.

[Event List](https://discover.usf.edu/portal/visit-international)

[International Student Support](https://www.usf.edu/world/international-student-support/about-us/contact-us.aspx) and the [Office of International Services](https://www.usf.edu/world/international-services/) offer additional sessions for all incoming students. Be sure to check [At-A-Glance Events List](https://www.usf.edu/world/international-student-support/engagement-and-events/events-and-workshops.aspx#Events) to receive valuable information on further steps to enroll at USF.

### Current Students

The International Student Support (ISS) Team offers numerous events and workshops,
and offers free-to-use space for your own organizations needs. Please see the information
below for our current schedule of events and workshops and on [BullsConnect](https://bullsconnect.usf.edu/USFWORLD/club_signup).

### Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

[Show What signature events does the International Student Support team host/assist?](https://www.usf.edu/world/international-student-support/engagement-and-events/events-and-workshops.aspx)

**Hurricane Awareness —** Have you ever experienced a Typhoon, Tropical Storm, Cyclone, or Hurricane? Do you
know how to prepare for Florida's extreme weather events? Learn how to stay safe and
prepare for Florida's Hurricane Season with the International Student Support Team!

**De-Stress Fest & Scam Prevention Week —** Whether you are a new student getting used to college life or overwhelmed and trying
to get through mid-term exams, the International Student Support Team is here with
relaxing activities to "De-Stress" from exams! Also, learn to avoid online scammers
during our simultaneous Scam Prevention Week!

**Bicycle, Pedestrian, and Auto Safety —** New environments are full of new adventures. However, with the state of Florida ranking
among the worst in Pedestrian and Auto safety, there is a reason to be cautious while
admiring our state. Join our Team as we give you tips and tricks on being safe as
a Bicyclist, Pedestrian, and Driver near Florida roadways, which can save your life!

**Graduating Cap Decorations —** As a graduating student, you can look forward to the future. The International Student
Support Team offers our graduating students free supplies to create cap decorations!
Students who deocrate their graduation caps can enter a [USF Cap Contest](https://www.usf.edu/commencement/cap-contest.aspx) to win prizes.

**Sash Ceremony —** International students graduating and being honored during [Commencement](https://www.usf.edu/commencement/) are able to participate in USF World's [Sash Ceremony](https://www.usf.edu/world/international-services/immigration/graduation.aspx). The ISS Team assists with the ceremony, offering students a once-in-a-lifetime way
to be recognized for their achievements, represent their country, and get a free country
flag sash!

[Show When can I know when the International Student Support Team hosts their events?](https://www.usf.edu/world/international-student-support/engagement-and-events/events-and-workshops.aspx)

You can check what upcoming events we will hold virtually and in-person on this web
page under the "At-A-Glance Events List" section.

Students can also check out when events happen on our [BullsConnect](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/bullsconnect/#:~:text=BullsConnect%20is%20USF's%20student%20engagement%20platform%20for%20all%20students%20on%20all%20campuses!) page under "[USF World](https://bullsconnect.usf.edu/USFWORLD/club_signup)", or by following our Instagram account [@usfworld\_iss](https://www.instagram.com/usfworld_iss/).

[Show Where is the Global Student Center on campus?](https://www.usf.edu/world/international-student-support/engagement-and-events/events-and-workshops.aspx)

The Global Student Center is located on the 1st level of the Faculty Administration
Office (FAO) building on the Tampa campus. The nearest entry ways are next to the
Social Science building (SOC) through either double-doors, or students can enter the
Ground level nearest to the 7B parking lot and walk up to the 1st level.

[Show Who operates the Global Student Center?](https://www.usf.edu/world/international-student-support/engagement-and-events/events-and-workshops.aspx)

The Global Student Center Center is a space shared by International Student Support
(ISS), International Admissions, Global Learning, and Peace Corps as of Fall 2025.
The departments are operated under USF World.
