### **USF TAMPA**

**![Three students walking by the University of South Florida sign on campus.](./Our Campuses _ Freshmen _ University of South Florida_files/why-usf-tampa-campus.jpg)**

USF's Tampa campus is a global research powerhouse in one of the country’s most desirable
metropolitan communities. From medicine and the arts to marine science and business,
students can access a broad range of degree and specialty programs. The Tampa campus
is USF’s largest campus location, and it serves undergraduate, graduate and doctoral
students.

Check out our playlist to learn more about the USF Tampa campus.

####

#### **Quick Facts:**

* Campus Size: Large
* Location: Tampa, FL - 4202 E Fowler Ave, Tampa, FL 33620
* [Residence Hall Options](https://www.usf.edu/housing/housing-options/index.aspx): Traditional Style, Suite Style, Apartment Style
* Nearby Attractions: Busch Gardens, Adventure Island Water Park, ZooTampa

#### **Exceptional Location**

In [close proximity](https://www.usf.edu/admissions/freshmen/why-usf/our-campuses/gulf-coast-living.aspx) to some of the best beaches in the U.S., attractions like theme parks, renowned museums,
performing arts centers and a booming business corridor, the Tampa campus is an ideal
college home.

#### **International Student Body**

Students at the Tampa campus hail from more than 145 countries around the world.

#### **Outstanding Student Life**

Our Tampa campus offers more than [600 clubs and organizations](https://bullsconnect.usf.edu/club_signup?category_tags=7174114&group_type=), award-winning recreation facilities (including an outdoor pool), an on-campus Publix
supermarket, our very own beach and frequent campus events.

#### **Academic Options**

More than [210 majors and concentrations are available](https://catalog.usf.edu/index.php?catoid=3), and high-achieving students enjoy accelerated programs, special housing, and career
and leadership development through the [Judy Genshaft Honors College](https://www.usf.edu/honors/).

#### **Home Away from Home**

Tampa campus students can take advantage of [numerous residence hall options](https://www.usf.edu/housing/housing-options/index.aspx), [living-learning communities](https://www.usf.edu/housing/residential-learning/llc.aspx) and [Greek Life](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/involvement/fraternity-and-sorority-life/) opportunities. You’ll also have access to our [many on-campus dining locations](https://usf.campusdish.com/LocationsAndMenus/Tampa), including Starbucks, Chick-fil-A, Panera Bread and more.

[Schedule a Tour](https://www.usf.edu/admissions/visit/tampa-campus-tour.aspx)

### **USF ST. PETERSBURG**

**![Students canoeing with their instructor on campus.](./Our Campuses _ Freshmen _ University of South Florida_files/why-usf-st-pete.jpg)**

At our waterfront [St. Petersburg campus](https://www.stpetersburg.usf.edu/about/index.aspx), you can pursue your degree in an inspiring atmosphere that encourages creativity.
Nestled along Florida’s Gulf Coast in vibrant downtown St. Petersburg, students enjoy
the best of Florida living: unparalleled scenic beauty within a culturally rich city.

Check out our playlist to learn more about the USF St. Petersburg campus.

#### **Quick Facts:**

* Campus Size: Mid-Sized
* Location: St. Petersburg, FL - 140 7th Ave S, St. Petersburg, FL 33701
* [Residence Hall Options](https://www.stpetersburg.usf.edu/student-life/housing/community-options/): Traditional Style, Suite Style, Apartment Style
* Nearby Attractions: Salvador Dali Museum, St. Pete Pier, Downtown St. Petersburg

#### **Downtown, Waterfront Campus**

Love the water? Put your feet in the sand between classes or hit the water on a kayak
or paddleboard. You'll be close to it all: internship opportunities, museums, restaurants,
open-air markets and concert venues are all just a short walk away. Some of the country’s
best beaches are just a quick drive (or SunRunner bus ride) away, too.

We also bring the St. Petersburg community to campus with our [Local Restaurant Row](https://usf.campusdish.com/LocationsAndMenus/StPetersburg/LocalRestaurantRow) dining option, allowing you to sample different St. Petersburg area restaurants on
campus each week!

#### **Academic Options**

Choose from [120+ majors and concentrations](https://www.stpetersburg.usf.edu/academics/degrees-and-programs/undergraduate-degrees.aspx), plus special opportunities in the [Judy Genshaft Honors College](https://www.usf.edu/honors/).

#### **Close-Knit Community**

With a low student-to-faculty ratio, our professors take a personal interest in helping
you achieve your dreams. In our supportive community, you won’t be just another face
in the crowd.

#### **Career Exploration**

[The Innovation Scholars Career Exploration Program](https://www.stpetersburg.usf.edu/student-life/career-center/students/innovation-scholars.aspx) provides unique job shadowing opportunities for incoming high-achieving first-year
students at the St. Petersburg campus, partnering them with companies and professionals
from across downtown St. Petersburg and within the St. Petersburg Innovation District.

#### **A Green Campus**

The St. Petersburg campus is proud of the key role students, staff and faculty play
as leaders and innovators in green initiatives. We’re recognized nationally as a university
[committed to sustainability.](https://www.stpetersburg.usf.edu/about/sustainability/index.aspx)

[Schedule a Tour](https://www.usf.edu/admissions/visit/st-petersburg-campus-tour.aspx)

### **usf SARASOTA-MANATEE**

**![Students in the main building on campus at USF's Sarasota-Manatee campus.](./Our Campuses _ Freshmen _ University of South Florida_files/why-usf-sarasota.jpg)**

Would you prefer a tight-knit community where your professors know you by name? You’ll
find that hometown feel on the [Sarasota-Manatee campus](https://www.sarasotamanatee.usf.edu/). The campus provides a personalized learning environment that allows you to engage
in unique, hands-on learning experiences through internships, research and service.

Check out our playlist to learn more about the Sarasota-Manatee campus.

####

#### **Quick Facts:**

* Campus Size: Small
* Location: Sarasota, FL - 8350 N. Tamiami Trail, Sarasota, FL 34243
* **[Residence Hall Options:](https://www.sarasotamanatee.usf.edu/housing/options/index.aspx "https://www.sarasotamanatee.usf.edu/housing/options/index.aspx")**  Suite Style, Apartment Style
* Nearby Attractions: The Ringling Museum, Bayfront Park, St. Armands Circle, University Town Center, Lido Beach

#### **Personalized Education**

With the lowest student-to-faculty ratio at USF, your professors know you by name
and have a keen interest in helping you achieve your goals.

#### **Academic Options**

Choose from [90+ majors and concentrations](https://www.sarasotamanatee.usf.edu/academics/undergraduate-programs-majors.aspx), plus special opportunities in the [Judy Genshaft Honors College](https://www.usf.edu/honors/).

#### **On-Campus Activities and Involvement**

Explore a variety of clubs and organizations, plus student recreation, such as a fitness
center, yoga and more.

#### **Growing Campus**

The Sarasota-Manatee campus is expanding with a Nursing-STEM building that will house
science labs and other innovative educational opportunities. [The first residence hall](https://www.sarasotamanatee.usf.edu/housing/) on the Sarasota-Manatee campus also opened in Fall 2024.

[Schedule a Tour](https://www.usf.edu/admissions/visit/sarasota-manatee-campus-tour.aspx)
