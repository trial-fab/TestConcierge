## Explore Your Resources

[![cover of USF World faculty passport resources featuring a green background and gold globe](./For Faculty _ Explore Your Resources _ USF World_files/passport-cover.png)](https://www.usf.edu/world/documents/faculty-passport.pdf)

Click the image to view the USF World Faculty Passport.

USF World is committed to linking faculty, students, and staff with [resources available](https://www.usf.edu/world/documents/gateway-global-engagement.pdf) across USF to promote and support international scholarship and global engagement.
Members of the larger USF community may access an array of resources from USF World
including information about [virtual global exchange](https://www.usf.edu/world/for-faculty/virtual-exchange/index.aspx), the [Fulbright Program](https://www.usf.edu/world/for-faculty/faculty-fulbright.aspx), [international travel](https://www.usf.edu/world/for-global-travelers/for-faculty-staff/index.aspx) policies, [hosting a visiting J-1 scholar](https://www.usf.edu/world/international-services/scholars-and-employees/hosting-a-scholar.aspx), assistance with your international [research & funding searches](https://www.usf.edu/world/for-faculty/research-funding.aspx), and USF's overall [global engagement](https://www.usf.edu/world/for-faculty/global-discovery-hub.aspx).

Take a look at USF's global footprint at the [Global Discovery Hub](https://www.usf.edu/world/for-faculty/global-discovery-hub.aspx). The Hub features USF's international partnerships and detailed information regarding
faculty activities across the globe. Search by country, region, institution, college,
department or keyword! More than 1,000 USF faculty profiles and 280 international
partnership agreements are accessible in the database. You can also access information
about student mobility and learn about USF's global partnership agreements.

Learn more about [sponsoring an agreement](https://www.usf.edu/world/for-partners/international-agreements/index.aspx) or [hosting an international delegation](https://www.usf.edu/world/for-faculty/hosting-delegation.aspx) as well in this section For Faculty.
