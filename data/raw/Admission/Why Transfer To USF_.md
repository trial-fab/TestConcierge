### Three Beautiful Campuses

Choose your unique journey at one of our three campuses! Each location includes all
the resources of the University of South Florida, a Preeminent Research University.
No matter which location you call home, you can attend classes at all three.

* **USF Tampa** is our largest campus, located in Tampa’s booming Uptown District.
* **USF St. Petersburg** is on the waterfront in artsy downtown St. Petersburg.
* **USF Sarasota-Manatee** is a tight-knit, coastal campus community in stunning Sarasota.

[Explore Our Campuses](https://www.usf.edu/admissions/transfer/why-usf/our-campuses/index.aspx)

### An Affordable Education

With one of the [lowest tuition rates in the country](https://tup-ofa.forest.usf.edu/cost_calculator/), generous [financial aid](https://usf.edu/financial-aid/apply/) and [scholarships](https://usf.edu/admissions/transfer/admission-information/cost-of-attendance/scholarships.aspx) and plenty of student employment opportunities, USF is an outstanding value. Even
if you live outside Florida, our tuition rates are competitive with many of your local
universities. Reach your education goals at a price that won’t break the bank.

[Calculate My Cost of Attendance](https://tup-ofa.forest.usf.edu/cost_calculator/)

### Athletics to Cheer About #HornsUp

Are you transferring from a school that *doesn't* have 21 NCAA Division I sports teams? Well get ready to jump for joy (just leave the
stunts to one of our [national championship-winning cheerleading teams](https://gousfbulls.com/sports/2017/2/21/211492094))! USF's athletes compete throughout the year, achieving excellence in football, basketball, softball
and more. During each season, USF students enjoy many perks, including:

* [Free admission to every game](https://gousfbulls.com/sports/2020/11/16/studenttickets) (including football)
* Fan rewards
* Free transportation to watch the Bulls play football at Raymond James Stadium, home
  of the Tampa Bay Buccaneers. Soon, students will cheer on the Bulls at [our very own on-campus stadium](https://www.usf.edu/stadium/).

[Get In the Game With USF Athletics](https://www.gousfbulls.com/)

### Your Career Action Plan

Are you already thinking about internships and employment? USF’s Career Services helps you identify, plan and achieve your career goals. Learn
how to self-assess, conduct career research, seek out experiences that will give you
transferable skills and search for full-time employment.

* **[Career Coaching](https://careers.usf.edu/channels/student/)** offers career exploration, resume writing guidance and more to help you discover
  your career interests and find employment.
* **[Cooperative education](https://careers.usf.edu/resources/cooperative-education-co-op/)** integrates paid, hands-on, career-related work experience through your academic program
  and provides you with valuable training in your chosen career field.
* **[Internships](https://careers.usf.edu/channels/search-for-a-job-internship/)** offer an opportunity to perform hands-on work in your desired field and are often
  shorter in duration than a co-op.
* **[Job search](https://careers.usf.edu/channels/student/)** prepares you for the post-graduation career search with a suite of services, including
  resume and cover letter preparation, employer research, networking tips, interview
  skills and tips on proper attire.

[Career Services](https://careers.usf.edu/)

[Back to Top](https://www.usf.edu/admissions/transfer/admission-information/why-usf.aspx#top)

### Award-Winning Veteran Support

At USF, military-connected students enjoy specialized support services; it's the least
we can do to thank you for your service. Ranked among the nation's [most veteran-friendly schools](https://www.usf.edu/news/2024/usf-earns-collegiate-purple-star-campus-designation-for-its-support-of-military-families.aspx), USF offers a number of special programs and resources for military-connected students,
including veterans, eligible dependents, active duty service members, and members of the Selected Reserve
on all three USF campuses. Find camaraderie among our large population of student veterans and receive ongoing
support from our [Office of Veteran Success](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/veterans/) as you earn a degree from our nationally-ranked university.

USF is one of the few universities in the nation with ROTC programs for all four military
branches. You can even join our [ROTC Living Learning Community](https://www.usf.edu/housing/residential-learning/llc-major/rotc.aspx), located in Maple Hall on the Tampa campus.

Explore our ROTC programs:

* [Army ROTC](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/army-rotc/)
* [Naval ROTC (including Marines)](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/naval-rotc/)
* [Air Force ROTC](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/air-force-rotc/index.aspx)
* [Joint Military Leadership Center](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/jmlc/index.aspx)

[Visit the Office of Veteran Success](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/veterans/)

[Back to Top](https://www.usf.edu/admissions/transfer/admission-information/why-usf.aspx#top)

### Ways to Play With Campus Recreation

From surfing to basketball to eSports, our over 70 [intramural](https://usf.edu/student-affairs/campus-rec/sports-programs/find-your-team.aspx) and [club sport](https://bullsconnect.usf.edu/club_signup?view=all&category_tags=7578239&group_type=) options allow you to find activities that interest you. Our other recreation and fitness offerings include:

* Gyms on all three campuses
* Equipment rentals on all three campuses
* Adventure Trips for all students
* Pools on the Tampa and St. Petersburg campuses
* Group fitness at the Tampa and St. Petersburg campuses
* Hiking trail on the Tampa campus
* USF Challenge Course on the Tampa campus
