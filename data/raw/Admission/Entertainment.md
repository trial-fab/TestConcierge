The University of South Florida (USF) is uniquely situated in three dinstinct communities
offering a variety of different entertainment, leisure, and fun activities to find
throughout the Tampa Bay area. For students looking to have fun outside of the classroom,
the following page lists nearby attractions, entertainment options, and leisure points
for students to partake in while studying at USF.

### Entertainment in Tampa

The USF Tampa has world-renowned entertainment options nearby campus that can accessed
via public transportation or private.

#### USF Riverfront Park

USF Riverfront Park has a variety of cheap rentals for students to use on the nearby
Hillsborough River, in addition to open fields for play, a ropes course for team and
single activities, and retreat to the natural outdoor environment.

[USF Riverfront Park](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/campus-rec/outdoors-and-aquatics/usf-riverfront-park.aspx)

---

#### Lettuce Lake Park

Lettuce Lake Park is a nearby nature preserve on the opposite side of the USF Riverfront
Park with scenic nature trails, Florida wildlife, and ecology.

[Lettuce Lake Park](https://hcfl.gov/locations/lettuce-lake-conservation-park)

####

---

#### Museum of Science and Industry (MOSI)

Museum of Science and Industry (MOSI) is an exciting entertainment destination for
students with families or children to learn about STEM sciences.

[Museum of Science and Industry (MOSI)](https://www.visittampabay.com/listings/mosi-museum-of-science-%26-industry/8961/)

####

---

#### Busch Gardens Tampa Bay

Busch Gardens Tampa Bay is a themepark with a variety of animals from around the world,
top-rated roller coasters, and events that span-year round.

#### Adventure Island

Adventure Island is a waterpark located nearby Busch Gardens for students who want
to relax in the sun, ride adventerous waterslides, and have fun with friends.

[Adventure Island](https://adventureisland.com/)

####

---

#### ZooTampa

ZooTampa is a local zoo situated in Lowry Park offers unique interactions with animals
from Florida and beyond.

[ZooTampa](https://zootampa.org/)
