## Advising

### first time in college students

[Exploratory Curriculum (ECM) program](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/programs/ecm/)

Not sure what to major in? The Exploratory Curriculum gives you a chance to explore
different careers before you officially declare a major. To get you started, Exploratory
Curriculum students are required to complete the ECM Udecide version of IDS2378, Foundations
of Interdisciplinary Knowledge and Inquiry, in their first Fall or Spring semester.

### Transfer Students

[High School or Entering Community College](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/transfer-student-success/fuse/index.aspx "Entering Community College or Finshishing High School")

Are you a high school student who plans on going to a community college for your Associate
degree then transferring to USF for a Bachelor’s degree? Look at the pathways you
can take to make it a smooth transition to USF. Usf.edu/fuse

[Office of Transfer Student Success](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/transfer-student-success/index.aspx "Office of Transfer Student Success")

Are you currently at a community college and want to see if USF is a fit for you to
get your
Bachelor’s degree? We have advisors at your college who would love to talk to you.
Find out more at [usf.edu/transfersuccess](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/transfer-student-success/index.aspx "Transfer success").

[Academic Programs](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/advisors/%2A%2A%2A%20Broken%20Link%20%2A%2A%2A "Academic Programs")

Students transfering with an AA or AS with 60 + credit hours.

### Current Students

[College Advising Offices](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/students/advising-offices.aspx "College Advising Offices")

Each college department has [advisors](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/students/advising-offices.aspx "College Advisors") to help guide you through your Plan of Study and serves as a resource to obtain your
degree. Plan on meeting with your advisor once a semester.
