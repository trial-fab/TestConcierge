## Academic Processes

[Academic Regulations Committee (ARC)](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/students/academic-regulations-committee/index.aspx)
Find information on Academic Regulations Committee (ARC) petition process including
guidelines, academic reinstatement, and forms.

[Change of Campus](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/documents/main/oneusf-changeofcampus-712020.docx "Change of Campus")
As of July 1st 2020, it is no longer necessary to receive approval from the Office
of Undergraduate Studies to process a change of campus (e.g., the change of institution
form is discontinued). [Read more](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/documents/main/oneusf-changeofcampus-712020.docx "Change of Campus").

[Degree Progression Policies](https://usf.app.box.com/v/usfpolicy10-505 "Degree Progression Policies")
Students may elect to graduate with two majors or two baccalaureate degrees to meet
their educational goals. Information for students choosing to pursue a double major
or dual degree can be found in this section.

[Exceptions for University Requirements](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/advisors/request-for-exceptions.aspx)
In the event any student experiences circumstances that prevent them from meeting
a university requirement for undergraduate graduation, students may work with their
academic advisor to request a wavier or substitution to satisfy the requirement.

[USF Policy/Regulation Exceptions and Appeals](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/advisors/usf-policy-regulation-exceptions-appeals.aspx)

This [request form](https://forms.office.com/r/Dcew0dvntx) is used when the student is requesting an exception to: allow more than 45 credits
from credit-by-exam, allow more than 14 non-degree seeking credits to be transferred
in. This request form is also used when the student is requesting an appeal to: college’s
decision on degree progression policy, college’s decision on academic disruption,
college’s decision on ARC petition. This form is also used to recommend a posthumous
or in memoriam degree. Please note: a college staff member must initiate the form
on behalf of the student.

[Leadership Studies Minor Declaration Form](https://usf.az1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_dgqP5C8VEqRqP5j)
Students who are interested in declaring the Leadership Studies minor should complete
this form and submit it to the Office of Undergraduate Studies either in person on
by email (email address located on the form).

> [Major Declaration](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/advisors/major-declaration.aspx "Major Declaration")Find information on how to declare a major.

[Major Reselection](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/undergrad-education/academic-enrichment/major-reselection.aspx "Major Reselection")
Check out information on changing majors due to reselection.

[Transient Enrollment/Cross Enrollment](https://www.usf.edu/registrar/services/non-degree-admission/transient_students.aspx "Transient Enrollment/Cross Enrollment")
Students wishing to take courses at an institution outside of the University of South
Florida must follow the Transient Enrollment/Cross Enrollment procedures listed in
this section.
