## New Faculty

Welcome to the University of South Florida! We are happy you have chosen the University
of South Florida to share your knowledge with students.

The Office of Undergraduate Studies' mission is to advance student inquiry and success
by fostering an inclusive and supportive learning environment. We provide services
and programming to assist students with pre-college preparation, academic progression
to graduation, and beyond.

Learn about the [departments](https://www.usf.edu/undergrad/about-us/ugs-departments.aspx "departments") within our office.

Below are the types of support and services we offer students.
