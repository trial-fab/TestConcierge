## Academic Advising Operations

# Mission

To elevate the quality, consistency, and effectiveness of academic advising across
the university by developing sustainable systems, delivering high-impact training,
and fostering a culture of continuous improvement.

# Vision

To be a nationally recognized leader in academic advising operations and unify the
advising communities at USF.

# Goals

1. Provide support to the Council for Academic Advising (CAA) to enhance the effectiveness,
   integration, and impact of academic advising on student success and institutional
   objectives.
2. Develop standardized support systems for academic advising training, practices, and
   processes to provide a foundation of exceptional academic advising service to students.
3. Identify and address the needs of the academic advisors across the colleges and campuses
   to streamline processes and operations.
4. Create, plan, and implement ongoing professional development initiatives for the academic
   advising community.
5. Ensure consistent review and assessment of academic advising initiatives which includes
   preparing reports and presentations for university stakeholders on advising performance
   and student success metrics.
