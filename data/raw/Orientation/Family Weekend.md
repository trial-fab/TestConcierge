## Family Weekend

![Family Weekend Save the Date for October 3-5, 2025](./Family Weekend_files/familyweekendbanner2025.png)

We are thrilled to host all of our USF Families for Family Weekend on **October 3-5, 2025**. Our newly revamped experience features fun events for our Bull families to enjoy.

[2025 Schedule](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/parents/engage/family-weekend-schedule.aspx)

[Family of the Year](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/parents/engage/family-of-the-year.aspx)

Family Weekend is open to families, friends, and USF students who attend any of our
three campus locations. **Registration is required for each participant, including your USF student, to receive
Family Weekend items and to participate in official weekend activities.** ***Registration closed September 14h, 2025, you can purchase tickets at the door for
$60.***

### FAQ

[Show When should we arrive to Family Weekend?](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/parents/engage/familyweekend.aspx)

Guests are encouraged to arrive on Friday morning in order to take full advantage
of the events being offered. We typically begin experiences on Friday afternoon around
1pm, all-day on Saturday, and leave Sunday open for families to navigate quality time
with their student and safe travels home.  **Start at Family Weekend Check-in!**

[Show What if my student has conflicts or campus obligations?](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/parents/engage/familyweekend.aspx)

We are thrilled that you and your student are excited about Family Weekend. Please
know that your student will likely still have class responsibilities, exams, and study
groups during the weekend. Some students also have organization commitments.

We encourage you and your student to discuss their academic schedule according to
their syllabi and any organization commitments prior to booking to ensure you have
a positive experience during the weekend. Unfortunately, we do not offer refunds for
these conflicts.

[Show What is the cost to attend Family Weekend?](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/parents/engage/familyweekend.aspx)

Tickets cost $50 for each family member including your USF student. Any child 5 years
of age or younger does not need a ticket to attend.

Once registration is closed, tickets will increase to $60 at the door.

[Show Where should we look for travel accomodations?](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/parents/engage/familyweekend.aspx)

The University of South Florida does not guarantee, approve, or endorse any specific
hotel, lodging, or transportation option. However, we encourage families while doing
their research to ask about a discounted rate for USF students and families. Guests
participating in the weekend are responsible for coordinating and covering costs associated
with their meals, travel, and accommodations.

[Local hotel guides & additional accomodation info](https://www.usf.edu/admissions/visit/tampa-campus-tour.aspx)

[Show Can I get a refund?](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/parents/engage/familyweekend.aspx)

We aim to provide the best experience for our students and family members participating
in our programs. Based on our event planning and purchasing timeline for Family  Weekend,
we are unable to provide refunds. ***All sales are final.***

[Show Where can I buy Football Game tickets with the Family Weekend discount?](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/parents/engage/familyweekend.aspx)

Join your fellow alumni, family, and friends returning to campus for the USF football
game on Friday, October 3rd, 2025. As a thank you for your support, we have an exclusive
family discount for our 2025 USF Family Weekend Football Game! ***You must register for Family Weekend to recieve the link.***

If you need help on purchasing tickets, please contact 1-800-Go-Bulls or email Bullstickets@usf.edu

**Accessibility:** If a member of your party needs a reasonable disability accommodation to participate
in Family Weekend please contact family@usf.edu.

**Add-on Experiences:** Registration does NOT include USF Football game tickets and other various add-on experiences;
please purchase directly from our campus partners.  You will receive discounted rates
for these add-on experiences including the Football game AFTER you register for the
weekend.

**Refund Policy:** We aim to provide the best experience for our students and family members participating
in our programs. Based on our event planning and purchasing timeline for Family  Weekend,
we are unable to provide refunds. ***All sales are final.***

**Thank you from Parent & Family Engagement!**
All of the financial support the Office of Parent & Family Engagement receives to
support our staffing, programs, and operations comes from the revenue generated by
Family Weekend. Your registration enables us to support families as they support their
student, to keep families informed of university happenings, and to provide opportunities
for families to connect with each other. Your contribution ensures that we can maintain
our programs and services while paving the way for new initiatives that will benefit
USF families for years to come. If you are able to further support the efforts of
the Office of Parent & Family Engagement, please consider making a gift that directly
supports services and experiences for you!

**Have Questions or Need Further Assistance?**
Please contact our office by emailing family@usf.edu.  We are happy to assist you!
