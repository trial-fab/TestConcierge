## CBCS Orientation Information

The College of Behavioral and Community Sciences welcomes you to the University of
South Florida! As a new student to the University and to the College, it is important
that you review each of the following steps below.

### Register for Orientation

Orientation is mandatory for all newly admitted USF students. If you have not already
you must register for an [Orientation](https://www.usf.edu/orientation/). If you do NOT intend to come to USF Tampa, you are in the wrong location, please
see [USF St. Petersburg](http://www.usfsp.edu/home/), or [USF Sarasota/Manatee](http://usfsm.edu/) as Orientation and degree requirements can differ from one another.

### Declare your major

Please submit the following [Orientation Declaration](https://intra2.cbcs.usf.edu/cbcs/advising/orientationDeclaration.php) form to bcs-ugstudentservices@usf.edu

### Schedule an Appointment with your Academic Advisor

See our [Advising page](https://www.usf.edu/cbcs/undergraduate/advising.aspx) for individual contact information as well as how to schedule an appointment.

To schedule an appointment, you will need your USF NetID, which is necessary for many
other activities as a new USF student. [Click here set up/activate your NetID and password](https://netid.usf.edu/).

### Register for Classes

You will not be able to Register for classes until you have been issues a time ticket
via the Registrar's office. You will get an email notification when you time ticket
has been issued.

* On-line USF [Schedule of Classes Search](https://www.usf.edu/registrar/register/)
* Use [Student Self-Service](https://my.usf.edu/) to register for classes
* Check your [Degree Works](http://degreeworks.usf.edu/) Audit
* What to do if you receive the error message "[CRN does not exist](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=i3PZQWfmORM&feature=youtu.be)" when you are trying to register for courses.
