### JOIN THE TEAM - BECOME AN ORIENTATION LEADER

Each year, the Office of Orientation looks for passionate and dedicated current undergraduate
students to join our team. Please check back soon for updates on 2024-2025 Orientation
Leader (OL) applications.

![A group photo of USF's Orientation team at the St. Petersburg campus.](./Contact USF St. Petersburg Orientation_files/sp-campus-orientation-image.png)

Applications for the Orientation Leader position will be available in late fall or
early spring 2025.

**Note:** If you are interested in being a Resident Assistant and an Orientation Leader at
the same time, please contact OrientationStPete@usf.edu.
