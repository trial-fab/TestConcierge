## Undergraduate Advising

If you have any questions throughout the day or have anything you would like to address
with us, please click this link, [BCS Advising Questions](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/meetup-join/19%3Ameeting_MzUyYmQ2NzktMTMxYy00ODUyLWIyYzMtZjgwMTRiZTk3YzMy%40thread.v2/0?context=%7b%22Tid%22%3a%22741bf7de-e2e5-46df-8d67-82607df9deaa%22%2c%22Oid%22%3a%22a94bbcb3-f020-451c-8ebc-3b7274aca3df%22%7d), you will be automatically redirected to a Microsoft Teams meeting. We will be constantly
monitoring this meeting so once you have entered the meeting, one of our members will
be in contact with you as soon as possible. If you don’t want a video call or audio
call, simply click the link to join the meeting and then go directly to the Teams
Chat for the meeting and ask away.

* Academic Advising appointments can be scheduled online with the [Archivum Appointment Scheduler](https://usf.appiancloud.com/suite/sites/archivum/page/archivum/report/daQEig) ([PDF instructions](https://www.usf.edu/cbcs/documents/advising/navigating-appointments-in-microsoft-teams-for-students.pdf)).  In advance of the appointment, you will receive an update to the schedule confirmation
  with Teams connection information.
* Emails:  We will continue to monitor and respond to student emails as quickly as possible.
    Please allow up to 48 business hours for a response.

### Mission

BCS advisors will utilize a holistic approach to empower students to develop and implement
an individualized plan for academic success, personal and career development. BCS
advisors will act to guide students on their academic journey throughout their tenure
at USF to help guide their career path and shape their life goals, until students
reach a successful and timely graduation.
