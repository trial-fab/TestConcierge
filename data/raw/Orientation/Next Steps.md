## Next Steps

New students who are able to obtain necessary immigration paperwork should arrive
to campus no later than the first week of the semester (add/drop week). Students must
complete their Immigration Document Check and enroll in courses. Failure to check
in on campus during the first week of classes and enroll will result in cancellation
of the admission application.

## **Click the link for your degree level to see your required next steps.**

[Show NEW UNDERGRADUATE STUDENTS - CLICK HERE](https://www.usf.edu/world/international-services/arrival-and-orientation/next-steps.aspx)

**Complete These Steps:**

1. Submit admissions deposit via your [MyBullsPath portal](https://admissions.usf.edu/myBullsPath).
2. Register for Orientation via your [MyBullsPath portal](https://admissions.usf.edu/myBullsPath). **Orientation is mandatory for each new student, and orientation will be online.**
3. Complete the Student Health Services (SHS) immunizations and insurance requirements
   and provide necessary paperwork. This will include having any existing holds cleared
   before you are able to register for classes. **Failure to comply with the USF Immunization policy by the *first day of class* could result in the cancellation of your classes.**
   1. Visit your [MyBullsPath portal](https://www.usf.edu/archive/orientation/reservation/dashboard.aspx) to determine which Student Health Services items are outstanding.
   2. If an item has a "T" or an "N" status code, you must take action to resolve the item.
      **If left unresolved, you will be unable to register for classes.**
      * Upload your documents via your [MyBullsPath portal](https://admissions.usf.edu/myBullsPath), through your [Student Self-Service (formerly OASIS)](https://my.usf.edu/), or via [web submission](http://form.jotformpro.com/form/32133984690964).
      * Declinations of recommended vaccines can be done via [MyBullsPath portal](https://admissions.usf.edu/myBullsPath), through your [Student Self-Service (formerly OASIS)](https://my.usf.edu/), or via [web submission](http://form.jotformpro.com/form/32133984690964).
4. Apply for housing via your [MyBullsPath portal](https://admissions.usf.edu/myBullsPath). You **must** also fill out [this survey](https://usf.az1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_4OzRY6UstIoXc1f) to indicate your housing preference.
5. Research [USF dining meal plans](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/msc/msc-services/usf-dining-services.aspx).
6. Log into [iStart](https://istart.usf.edu/) to complete your pre-arrival tasks.
7. **Obtain your official and final academic documents and bring them with you for "doc
   check" upon your arrival to campus.** You may also have a school counselor email the final documents (do not include any
   immigration or financial documents – these are uploaded to iStart) to the International
   Admission office at international@usf.edu for processing.

[Show NEW GRADUATE STUDENTS - CLICK HERE](https://www.usf.edu/world/international-services/arrival-and-orientation/next-steps.aspx)

**Complete These Steps:**

1. Complete the Student Health Services (SHS) immunizations and insurance requirements
   and provide necessary paperwork. This will include having any existing holds cleared
   before you are able to register for classes. **Failure to comply with the USF Immunization policy by the *first day of class* could result in the cancellation of your classes.**
   1. To view your immunization and health insurance status, log in to your [Student Self-Service (formerly OASIS)](http://www.myusf.edu/). Visit USF’s [Immunization Compliance webpage](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/student-health-services/immunizations/index.aspx), for an explanation of required and optional vaccines and instructions on how to
      submit your documentation to our office. Visit USF’s [International Insurance Compliance webpage](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/student-health-services/insurance/inscompliance.aspx), for instructions on how to release the Student Insurance hold and information on
      how to purchase the USF policy or waive out with an adequate alternative policy.
2. Apply for on-campus housing at <https://www.usf.edu/housing/housing-application/index.aspx> or find off-campus housing at <https://offcampushousing.usf.edu/>
3. Research [USF dining meal plans](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/msc/msc-services/usf-dining-services.aspx).
4. Log into [iStart](https://istart.usf.edu/) to complete your pre-arrival tasks.
5. Sign up for Graduate Student Orientation - <https://www.usf.edu/graduate-studies/students/newly-admitted-students/orientation/new-graduate-student-orientation.aspx>  (note: they post the date very late)
