## Graduate Student Week of Welcome

Graduate Student Week of Welcome is a week-long celebration to welcome new and returning
graduate students each fall and spring. Encouraged by the Office of Graduate Studies
(OGS), this celebration features a series of events for Graduate Bulls to get involved,
making lasting connections to start to their graduate careers. *Welcome to USF!*

Please share this list of activities with other graduate students and visit the Office
of New Student Connection's Week of Welcome (WOW) website to learn about [additional campus-wide events](https://www.usf.edu/student-affairs/new-student/students/wow-fifty-days.aspx) provided for USF students on each campus. All university-sponsored events are open
to graduate students.
